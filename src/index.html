Simple chess engine written in TypeScript. For now, there is a rudimentary AI that makes decisions mostly randomly. If it has an opportunity to capture a piece, it will, no matter what. If it can capture multiple pieces, it will gnab the one with the highest points value.

  • Both sides are human-controlled by default.
  • To activate the AI, click on the team you want it to control.
  • Click that team again to disengage it.

This is simply my way of figuring out chess for TypeScript, it's not intended to be redistributable. If you want a library that is redistributable to build your own chess game, use the official Chessboard.js. If you want to see what this thing can do because you're curious, poke away.
